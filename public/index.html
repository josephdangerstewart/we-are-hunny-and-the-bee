When we're getting married

November 28th, 2020

Hello family, friends, and loved ones! Thank you for coming to our wedding website. We have invested a lot of time and energy into this project and we are blessed for your willingness to experience it. We hope you all have enjoyed just a small snippet of our story!

However, we are planning on having a much bigger celebration with all of our family, friends, and loved ones whenever conditions with the pandemic improve.

For now, we would love to thank each and every single person who has been a part of our journey since the start. We thank you for your love and support, showing us the grace and love of God in all that you’ve done for us. Though we are sadden to not be able to celebrate with you all now, we deeply look forward to the day when we can all celebrate together in person. We love you all so much and we are so excited to see you all again soon.

May God bless you and remind you of His deep and genuine love for you today!
